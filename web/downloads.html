Download Trade Guild Ledger for Elder Scrolls Online

Trade Guild Ledger needs two things to work. The addon which needs to be placed in your addons folder for ESO and also a client which takes data from the addon and uploads it to the website. Users who contribute data power this website.

Client

The client keeps your addon up to date and automatically syncs listings you see in game to the Trade Guild Ledger website.

To download the Windows client please click here

Addon

There is no in game UI currently. The addon only reads listings you see in the game and uploads them to the Trade Guild Ledger website.

The addon should be placed under ~\Documents\Elder Scrolls Online\live\AddOns

To download the addon please click here
